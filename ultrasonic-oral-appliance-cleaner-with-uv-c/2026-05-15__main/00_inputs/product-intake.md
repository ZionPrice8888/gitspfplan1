# Product Intake

## Core

- Brand: Orivati
- Product name: Ultrasonic Oral Appliance Cleaner with UV-C
- Product keyword: ultrasonic retainer cleaner
- Market: US market, English
- Output language: English
- Product description: A compact ultrasonic cleaning device using 42kHz ultrasonic waves and UV-C light to deeply clean retainers, night guards, dentures, aligners, and dental trays — removing bacteria, odor, and buildup that brushing and cleaning tablets miss in the grooves.
- How the product helps customers: Helps oral appliance wearers eliminate hidden odor and bacteria from retainers, night guards, and dentures without harsh chemicals or manual scrubbing — so they never feel embarrassed about a "clean-looking but foul-smelling" appliance again.
- Core problem: People who wear retainers, night guards, or dentures can't get them truly clean — they look clean but smell disgusting, and cleaning tablets only mask the odor without reaching the microscopic grooves where bacteria hide.
- Target customer demographic: Adults 25-60 who wear retainers, night guards, or dentures; orthodontic patients; denture caregivers for elderly parents; people embarrassed about removing their appliance in public or near a partner because of smell.

## Product Details

- Features: 42kHz ultrasonic cleaning; UV-C sterilization; 3 cleaning modes (gentle/standard/deep); 180ML capacity; auto shut-off timer; quiet operation; works for retainers, night guards, dentures, aligners, jewelry
- Materials/specs: BPA-free ABS plastic tank, 180ML capacity, 25W ultrasonic transducer, USB-C charging port, removable basket, UV-C LED lamp rated 10,000+ hours
- How it works: The 42kHz ultrasonic waves create millions of microscopic cavitation bubbles that penetrate every groove and crevice of the dental appliance, dislodging bacteria and biofilm that brushes and tablets can't reach. The UV-C light then sterilizes the surface. User simply places appliance in the basket, adds water, selects mode, and presses start.
- Unique mechanism candidates: "Deep-Groove Ultrasonic Purge System" — emphasizing that ultrasonic cavitation reaches where tablets and brushes physically cannot; "Smell-Test Proven Clean" — positioning around the emotional shame of a smelly retainer
- Claims allowed: Can say it helps clean oral appliances more thoroughly than tablets alone; helps remove odor-causing bacteria from hard-to-reach grooves; UV-C supports hygienic cleanliness; suitable for retainers, night guards, dentures, aligners
- Claims to avoid: Do NOT claim it kills 99.99% bacteria, prevents disease, cures oral infections, or replaces professional dental cleaning. Avoid "sterilizes" in medical sense — use "helps clean" and "supports hygiene"
